What is this?

Pomodoro is a workflow technique, where you have bursts of productivty with small breaks, then after a certain duration, you have an extended break. This is to help alleviate either burning yourself out from long intense sessions or the exact opposite, not concentrating enough!

The most common implementation and used on this site is as follows:

  1. 25 minutes of work
  2. 5 minute break
  3. Repeat four times, after which you have a 10 minute break
  4. Start a new Pomodoro

Your pomodori (1 interval) count is displayed in the top left hand corner.

For more extensive information please see the Pomodoro technique Wikipedia entry.
